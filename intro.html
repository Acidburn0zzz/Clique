Introduction¶

Overview¶

Clique is an implementation of the multifrontal algorithm for symmetric and Hermitian systems which is designed to be scalable (both in terms of wall time and memory usage) and as easy to use as possible. As of now, Clique supports both \(LDL^T\) and \(LDL^H\) factorizations (without pivoting), so, in addition to symmetric/Hermitian positive-definite systems, Clique can also handle indefinite systems which do not require pivoting. Support for threshold-pivoted LU factorizations will be added in a future release.

Dependencies¶

Clique is built on top of Elemental and ParMETIS, and both are distributed with Clique. Clique keeps all of Elemental’s dependencies except for PMRRR.
